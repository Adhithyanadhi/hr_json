Influence of Prosopis juliflora on tank system

Here we elaborated on the effect of P.juliflora on tank systems by studying a case study in Ramanathapuram district, we created a vulnerability map of tank system from P.juliflora. We had also explained, pictorially, the influence on tank systems with and without of P.Juliflora. Lastly, we provided suggestions to overcome the effects of P.Juliflora in Tank system.
